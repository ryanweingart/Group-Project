Disclaimer:

This application is in its beta phase. Find My Fit is working on improving the experience and produce that we are aiming to provide. Find My Fit aims to show prospective students a sample of the school options that they have available to them, based on their responses to this beta phase quiz. Find My Fit encourages prospective students to follow up our recommendations with addequate research before making a decision on which school they untimately attend.
